Conservation Insights

At Bash Tours & Travel, we don't just guide safaris; we monitor the health of our ecosystems. Our data insights show a steady 3% growth in the Chimpanzee populations of Budongo Forest over the last five years. This research helps us tailor our tours to be as low-impact and high-value as possible for both the traveler and the environment.

Using professional mapping and data tracking, we provide our clients with the most accurate times for wildlife sightings in Queen Elizabeth and Murchison Falls.
